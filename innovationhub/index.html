About InnovationHub

InnovationHub by Orion focuses on fostering innovation and problem-solving skills in the scientific field. Directed towards young minds in primary, secondary, and tertiary education, our aim is to start clubs in schools, particularly in robotics.

We believe that by engaging students in hands-on projects, we can ignite their passion for technology and empower them to become the innovators of tomorrow. Our approach encourages collaboration and teamwork, enabling students to share ideas and learn from each other.

Through mentorship from industry professionals, we provide guidance and support that inspires creativity and critical thinking.

Together, we aspire to create a vibrant community where young minds can thrive, explore new possibilities, and turn their dreams into reality.
